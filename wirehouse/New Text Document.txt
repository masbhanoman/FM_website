Warehouse Features

WAC warehouse in Dhaka is adjacent to the cargo terminal of international airport and provides a large storage facility for all kinds of cargo. The experience staffs with modern equipment supervise movements of freight in and out of the warehouse.

The Warehouse facility can be summarized as:

Area: 6000 sq.ft.
24 hours security
Sort and segregating
Storage and Distribution
Special document / package storage
Personal / Household effect storage
Packing
Container stuffing / un-stuffing
